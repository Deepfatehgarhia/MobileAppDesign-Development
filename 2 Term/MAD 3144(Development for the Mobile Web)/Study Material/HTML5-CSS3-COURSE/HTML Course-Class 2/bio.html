About Me

I live in São Paulo but I serve clients from all over the world. I am known for making quality , durability products and adding value to my customers.

I work using the web as a platform, that is, I breathe HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript (or better: ECMASCript). I create websites for everyone, following the main accessibility, responsiveness and semantic web policies, without neglecting the quality of code.

How to work

Satisfy my customers is a priority. For this, I guarantee a highly interactive development process, based on continuous feedback. I do not work with a closed scope : the client decides when the product is ready.

I also do not work with deadlines: quality is too important to be sacrificed .

Experience

I have already developed projects for large companies such as BMW, Spotfy and IBM. In them, the main focus was to deliver an immersive and impactful experience to the end user without neglecting the page's performance and accessibility.

I've also been hired to turn large portals, like The New York Times, into responsive pages. I was responsible for refurbishing the layout, rearranging the content, and rewriting the code in a more reusable way.

Community
